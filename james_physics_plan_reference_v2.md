### Session 1 — LC Section A format familiarisation
**Timing:** Week 1 (22–28 June) · ~55 min · any free morning
**Tool:** examinations.ie

Download LC Physics HL papers from 2023, 2022, and 2019. Also download the official List of Experiments from examinations.ie — this is the SEC-published document specifying exactly which experiments can appear in Section A. Read the list first so you know which experiments are in scope, then read Section A questions alongside the marking schemes. Identify the template: apparatus, procedure, precautions, sources of error, graph/results. Build a one-page reference card with five headings (Apparatus / Procedure / Precautions / Sources of Error / Graph). This card is the template for every Section A answer written in 6th year.

**What Section A rewards:**
- Specific equipment names (metre rule not ruler, retort stand not stand)
- Numbered procedure steps
- Precautions linked to accuracy improvement
- Sources of error linked to why they affect the measurement
- Axes labelled with units on all graphs

## LC paper structure (reference)

**Section A — Experiments (~120 marks):** 3 questions, answer all 3. Fixed template: apparatus, procedure, precautions, sources of error, graph/results. Marks go to specific terminology in specific places.

**Section B — Theory and problems (280 marks):** 6 questions, answer any 5. Each question worth 56 marks. Mix of definitions, written explanations, and calculations. Command words: state (one sentence fact), explain (fact + reason), describe (sequence or process).

**Total written exam:** 400 marks (120 Section A + 280 Section B) = 60% of LC Physics grade.
**AAC project:** 200 marks = 40% of LC Physics grade.
